“SHUT THE BOX”

A round of the game consists of each player taking one turn to try to shut the 
box. Winner will be the one who shuts all flips first in each round  
 
Each player has to  
 
1. Roll 2 dice and add up the number 
 
2. Flip the number tiles that add up to the total number of dice  
(minimum 1 and maximum 2 flips at a time) 
 
3. Continues rolling dice and flipping number tiles until you can’t flip 
any tiles. Your turn is over. The sum of the remaining open tiles is 
your score. After each player finished their turn, the winner is the 
player with lowest score  
 
Example:  
If the total is 8 (Dice count), the player may choose one of the following options 
(minimum 1 and maximum 2 flips at a time) to shut the flip or flips: 
 8 
 7 & 1 
 6 & 2 
 5 & 3 
Assuming that one of the options is available to be played, the player selects 
one, covers the selected number or numbers and proceeds to throw the dice 
again.
