 SAMVEEKSHANA-2026    
“TECHNICAL QUIZ” 
Date of event: 11/03/2026 
Time: 11:00 am to 02:00 pm  
The basic rule of the competition:  
1. Only team entries are allowed, and team shall consist of two persons. 
There is no limit of teams from single institution.  
2. The participants shall not be allowed to use mobile or other electronic 
instruments during the quiz time.  
3. The questions shall be in the form of multiple choice, true/false statement, 
specific answer question etc…  
4. Replacement of any participant of a team is not allowed after the 1st round.  
5. In case of tie between 2 or more teams, further 5 questions will be asked 
for final selection. This step will be continued till the final round. 
6. Response time for each question is 60 seconds.  
7. Screening will be done based on the number of registration. 
There will be six rounds in the competition  
The following rules will be applicable for the rounds. 
1. Round 01 to Round-03: visual questions/photo identification/basic civil 
engineering questions.  
2. Elimination of 2 Teams based on the lowest score in the previous 3 rounds 
3. Round-04 & Round-05: Non-Technical fun rounds/Technical level -01 
questions. 
4. Elimination of 3 teams based on the lowest score in the previous 2 rounds 
5. Round-06 Technical Level-02 questions.  
6. Winner will be announced based on highest marks from round 01 to round 
06. 
 
 Each round will have 2 sets of questions for each team. 
 From Round -01 to Round -04 each question will carry 0 5 Mark. 
(including fun rounds) 
 Round-05 and Round-06 each question will carry 10 Mark.   
 Passing the Question is allowed from Round 5.    
 1st pass question answered team will be given last preference for next 
pass question – each pass question carries 05 Marks.
